Leadership

David Enriquez, CEO

David Enriquez brings over 20 years of hands-on experience in the warehouse racking and repair industry. Over two decades, he has designed, installed, and managed countless repair and safety solutions for facilities across North America.

Mentored by some of the best in the industry, David’s approach combines engineered precision with real-world practicality, always centered on safety and long-term performance.

To expand capabilities and serve customers end-to-end, David acquired FBS Group, Inc.—a respected provider of warehouse equipment solutions. This strategic step strengthens Elite Rackz’ supply, installation, and service offering while reinforcing the company’s commitment to quality and speed.

Beyond the job site, David is a devoted family man with a wonderful wife and children. He leads with humility, accountability, and a constant drive to improve—always striving for greatness.

Jacob Alvarez, Director

Jacob Alvarez plays a key role in developing Elite Rackz’ customer relationships, project coordination, and field strategy. With over 5 years of experience in warehouse rack repair, Jacob works directly with facilities across the country to identify damage, develop repair strategies, and deliver solutions that reduce downtime while improving safety.

Known for his hands-on approach and deep industry knowledge, Jacob focuses on helping customers navigate the complexities of rack damage, engineering requirements, and installation logistics—ensuring every project moves from assessment to completion efficiently.

His work helps bridge the gap between engineering, manufacturing, and field operations, supporting Elite Rackz’ mission of delivering smarter rack repair solutions without compromise.

Rosalie York, Office Mgr.

Rosalie York brings 7 years of warehouse and rack repair experience to Elite Rackz. As Office Manager, she keeps day-to-day operations organized and responsive—supporting scheduling, coordination, and internal follow-through that helps projects run smoothly.

Rosalie is known for her reliability and attention to detail, helping ensure communication stays clear and projects stay organized as they move through each stage.

The people closest to her matter most—especially her family, including the newest addition: her grandbaby. Outside of work, Rosalie enjoys spending time with family, watching NASCAR, soccer, and tennis, and she never misses a good crime scene documentary.
