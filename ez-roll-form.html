Overview

EZ Roll-Form Repair Kits are designed to restore damaged roll-form rack columns while preserving beam adjustability and supporting multiple protection configurations. These kits can reuse existing factory bracing when possible or accept new bracing when needed.

Best For

  • Roll-form rack damage requiring an engineered repair path
  • Narrow aisles, wider aisles, or VNA conditions depending on deflector choice
  • Facilities prioritizing beam adjustability and repair budget efficiency
Three configuration options

Available with non-protruding, protruding, or no deflector depending on site conditions.

Beam-friendly design

Allows maximum beam adjustability and can reuse undamaged factory strutting when possible.

Budget-conscious repair option

A strong repair solution for roll-form systems without the heavier double- or single-leg kit approach.

Configurations

Choose the right deflector style

Non-Protruding Deflector

This reinforced column kit offers ultimate repair and protection against outrigger impact damage, the most common cause of frame damage. Made of the same punch style as the parent frame.

Recommended for narrow aisles due to 1/4″ deflector protrusion. It accommodates most beam levels above 12”, reuses existing factory bracing or accepts new bracing when needed, and reinforces the front post with a backer channel.

Available with V-nose, non-protruding, or internal deflectors for added bottom-column protection. Includes a massive 3/8” thick oversized footplate with two 3/4” x 7” anchors for high impact resistance and minimal twisting.

Protruding Deflector

This reinforced column kit offers ultimate repair and protection against outrigger impact damage, the most common cause of frame damage. Made of the same punch style as the parent frame.

Recommended for wider aisles due to 1″ deflector protrusion. It accommodates most beam levels above 12”, reuses existing factory bracing or accepts new bracing when needed, and reinforces the front post with a backer channel for additional strength.

Without Deflector

This Pallet Rack Repair Kit returns any frame post to original specifications. The Frontline Repair Kit is best for VNA and rear post applications.

It reuses existing factory bracing or accommodates new bracing when needed. It accommodates nearly all beam heights while maximizing your repair budget, allows for maximum beam adjustability, and can reuse undamaged factory strutting.

An additional backer is added for increased impact resistance.
